OpenLoco v26.03.1 is out! Yeah, only one day after v26.03. Remember how we announced we had
[improved entity invalidation](https://openloco.io/news/2026/03/openloco-v26.03.html#improved-entity-invalidation)?
Turns out there was a gaping bug that would occur when the viewport is rotated. Oops.
@ZehMatt quickly patched the issue, but obviously we wish we had caught this before the release.

Please find the [download](https://openloco.io/download/) on our website, or read the
[changelog](https://github.com/OpenLoco/OpenLoco/blob/v26.03.1/CHANGELOG.md) on GitHub.
