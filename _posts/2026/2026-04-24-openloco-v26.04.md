## Localisation improvements

@erdikayaTR has improved the Turkish localisation, adding translations for newer strings and clarifying older ones
[(#3712)](https://github.com/OpenLoco/OpenLoco/pull/3712).

Like Locomotion, OpenLoco support custom keyboard shortcuts. @LeeSpork noticed one of the lesser used modifier keys,
the `Menu` key, was not being localised. This has now been fixed [(#3467)](https://github.com/OpenLoco/OpenLoco/pull/3467).
